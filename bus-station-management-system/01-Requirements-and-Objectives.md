# Bus Station Management System (Case Study)
## Requirements and Objectives

---

## What are the requirements?

### 1. Bus Management
- **Bus Registration and Classification**
  - Bus registration (bus ID, license plate, model, capacity, manufacturer)
  - Bus classification (standard, luxury, express, mini-bus)
  - Bus maintenance scheduling and tracking
  - Bus status management (active, maintenance, retired)

- **Route Management**
  - Route registration and mapping
  - Route classification (local, intercity, interstate, international)
  - Route scheduling and timetables
  - Route pricing and fare management

- **Trip Management**
  - Trip planning and scheduling
  - Trip assignment to buses and drivers
  - Real-time trip tracking
  - Trip cancellation and rescheduling

### 2. Human Resources Management

- **Passenger Management**
  - Passenger registration (passenger ID, name, address, phone number, email)
  - Ticket booking and reservation
  - Ticket cancellation and refund processing
  - Passenger history and preferences tracking

- **Staff Management**
  - Staff registration (staff ID, name, role, department, contact information)
  - Driver management (license validation, schedule assignment, performance tracking)
  - Ticketing staff management
  - Maintenance staff management
  - Administrative staff management
  - Staff scheduling and shift management

### 3. Financial Management
- **Revenue Management**
  - Ticket sales tracking
  - Payment processing (cash, card, mobile payment)
  - Revenue reporting and analytics
  - Refund processing

- **Expense Management**
  - Fuel cost tracking
  - Maintenance cost tracking
  - Staff salary management
  - Operational expense tracking

### 4. Operational Management
- **Platform Management**
  - Platform/bay assignment
  - Platform availability tracking
  - Platform scheduling

- **Security Management**
  - Access control
  - Surveillance monitoring
  - Incident reporting

---

## Objectives

### Primary Objectives

1. **Efficiently organize and manage bus operations and resources**
   - Real-time bus tracking and status monitoring
   - Optimal route planning and scheduling
   - Efficient bus allocation and utilization
   - Maintenance scheduling and tracking
   - Resource cataloging and archiving

2. **Enhance passenger access to services**
   - User-friendly booking system
   - Multiple booking channels (online, mobile, counter)
   - Real-time schedule information
   - Seat selection and preferences
   - Notification system for delays and cancellations

3. **Efficiently keep track of passenger bookings and transactions**
   - Complete booking history
   - Transaction tracking and reporting
   - Payment verification and reconciliation
   - Refund processing and tracking

4. **Tracking of staff activities and operations**
   - Driver performance and compliance tracking
   - Staff attendance and scheduling
   - Operational activity logging
   - Performance metrics and reporting

### Secondary Objectives

5. **Improve operational efficiency**
   - Reduce waiting times
   - Optimize route utilization
   - Minimize operational costs
   - Improve resource allocation

6. **Enhance customer satisfaction**
   - Provide accurate and timely information
   - Streamline booking and check-in processes
   - Improve service quality
   - Handle complaints and feedback effectively

7. **Ensure compliance and safety**
   - Regulatory compliance tracking
   - Safety protocol enforcement
   - Incident management
   - Audit trail maintenance

8. **Data-driven decision making**
   - Generate comprehensive reports
   - Analyze passenger patterns
   - Optimize routes and schedules
   - Forecast demand and capacity planning
